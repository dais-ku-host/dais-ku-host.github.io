Join DAIS Lab

Data & Adaptive Intelligence Systems Lab, Korea University

Culture

Our mission is to empower innovation through data-driven insights, fostering intelligence systems with adaptive capabilities. We pursue this mission through high-quality research and a collaborative, respectful culture.

Roles and Expectations

Undergraduate Students

  • Explore and study research topics you are interested in
  • Participate in research projects, analyze existing works and/or develop your new ideas
  • Encouraged to submit a paper to domestic conferences (e.g., Korea Computer Congress) or prepare a paper for the top venues

Graduate Students

  • Design the research goal that you will pursue in your graduate studies
  • Find and develop research ideas to submit papers at the top venues*
  • Participate in research projects with domestic/global academia/industry that are aligned with your research goal
  • Publish a strong, consistent thesis on your research goal
  • It is highly encouraged to do an internship before starting graduate studies

Post-docs / Researchers

  • Lead your research projects and publish papers at the top venues
  • Mentor and collaborate with interns and graduate students if preferred

*Target top venues: Data Science (KDD, WWW, SIGIR, CIKM, ICDM, etc.), Natural Language Processing (ACL, EMNLP, etc.), Artificial Intelligence / Machine Learning (NeurIPS, ICML, AAAI, ICLR, etc.), and relevant high-ranked journals.

What We Would Expect from You

  • Be passionate about what you study
  • Be respectful to whom you work with
  • Be responsible for what you do

What You Could Expect from Us

  • Conducting a high-quality research project and publishing top-tier papers
  • Research-focused culture and working environments
  • Encouragement for active collaborations within/outside the team

How to Apply

If you are interested in joining DAIS Lab, please send an email to susik{@}korea.ac.kr with your CV and a brief statement of your research interests.
